# CS50 AI - Introduction to Artificial Intelligence with Python

## Course Overview
CS50 AI explores the concepts and algorithms at the foundation of modern artificial intelligence. Students dive into the ideas that give rise to technologies like game-playing engines, handwriting recognition, and machine translation through hands-on projects and Python programming.

## üìã Course Information
- **Duration**: 7 weeks
- **Time Commitment**: 10-20 hours per week
- **Difficulty**: Intermediate
- **Prerequisites**: CS50x or equivalent programming experience

## üóìÔ∏è Weekly Structure

### Week 0: Search
- Problem-solving through search algorithms
- **Topics**: Depth-first search, breadth-first search, A* search
- **Projects**: Degrees (Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon), Tic-Tac-Toe

### Week 1: Knowledge
- Knowledge representation and reasoning
- **Topics**: Propositional logic, inference, resolution
- **Projects**: Knights (Logic puzzles), Minesweeper

### Week 2: Uncertainty
- Dealing with uncertainty in AI
- **Topics**: Probability, Bayes' Rule, Bayesian networks
- **Projects**: PageRank, Heredity (Genetic inheritance)

### Week 3: Optimization
- Mathematical optimization techniques
- **Topics**: Local search, simulated annealing, constraint satisfaction
- **Projects**: Crossword (Constraint satisfaction)

### Week 4: Learning
- Machine learning fundamentals
- **Topics**: Supervised learning, k-nearest neighbors, perceptron
- **Projects**: Shopping (Predicting purchases), Nim (Reinforcement learning)

### Week 5: Neural Networks
- Deep learning and neural networks
- **Topics**: Artificial neural networks, backpropagation, TensorFlow
- **Projects**: Traffic (Convolutional neural networks)

### Week 6: Language
- Natural language processing
- **Topics**: Syntax, semantics, context-free grammars, n-grams
- **Projects**: Parser (Sentence parsing), Questions (Question answering)

## üß† AI Concepts Covered

### Search Algorithms
- **Uninformed Search**: DFS, BFS, uniform-cost search
- **Informed Search**: A*, greedy best-first search
- **Adversarial Search**: Minimax, alpha-beta pruning

### Knowledge Representation
- **Propositional Logic**: Boolean satisfiability
- **First-Order Logic**: Predicates and quantifiers
- **Inference**: Resolution, forward/backward chaining

### Machine Learning
- **Supervised Learning**: Classification, regression
- **Unsupervised Learning**: Clustering, dimensionality reduction
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Q-learning, policy gradients
- **Deep Learning**: CNN, RNN, transformer architectures

### Probability & Statistics
- **Bayesian Networks**: Probabilistic reasoning
- **Hidden Markov Models**: Sequential data
- **Monte Carlo Methods**: Sampling techniques
